Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get verified as a teacher?
Teacher verification usually takes 24-48 hours. Our admin team reviews all documents and qualifications before approving your profile.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, Netbanking, and Wallets through Razorpay. All payments are secure and encrypted.
Can I get a refund for unused points?
Points are non-refundable but never expire. You can use them anytime to unlock tutor contacts or student requests.
How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll need to answer your security question to reset your password.
Is there any fee for posting tuition requests?
No, posting tuition requests is completely free for students. You only pay points when you want to unlock teacher contact details.
